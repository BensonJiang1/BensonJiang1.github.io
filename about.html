About Me

I am a student at Tufts University, pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics. My academic journey has equipped me with strong problem-solving skills and a solid foundation in algorithms, data structures, and mathematical reasoning. I enjoy exploring the intersection of theory and practical applications, always looking for ways to apply my knowledge to solve real-world challenges.

Passion for RL, ML, and SWE

My interests are in Reinforcement Learning, Machine Learning, and Software Engineering. I enjoy developing intelligent systems that can learn from data and improve over time. Whether it's building AI-driven applications, optimizing decision-making processes, or designing scalable software architectures, I find deep satisfaction in applying cutting-edge technologies to meaningful problems. I am particularly interested in AI applications in areas like healthcare, finance, and robotics.

Research & Extracurriculars

Beyond academics, I have actively contributed to research projects in Reinforcement Learning, working on topics like strategic decision-making, nonstationary environments, and neural network-based optimization. My work has given me hands-on experience with advanced machine learning techniques, experimentation, and data analysis.

In addition, I am a full-stack developer at JumboCode, a student-led organization where I collaborate with teams to develop software solutions for non-profits. Through this experience, I have honed my ability to work in agile development environments, participate in code reviews, and contribute to impactful projects that serve the community.
