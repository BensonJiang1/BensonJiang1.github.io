NEUR-LOOP

Description:

   This research paper explores the intersection of Reinforcement Learning (RL) and neural signals, investigating how brain-inspired AI models can enhance our understanding of decision-making processes. The study examines how neural activity correlates with RL-driven behaviors, analyzing patterns in reward-based learning and adaptation. By leveraging computational models and neuroscience data, the paper provides insights into how RL principles can be applied to interpret neural dynamics, potentially contributing to advancements in AI-driven brain-computer interfaces and cognitive neuroscience.
